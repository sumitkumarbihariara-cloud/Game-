About NECPL
Our Mission & Vision
Building Northeast India's most respected cricket platform — from grassroots to glory.
🎯

Our Mission

To create a professional cricket platform in Northeast India that identifies, nurtures, and promotes talented players by providing high-quality competitive matches, fair opportunities, and transparent selection processes.

  • Develop young cricket talent from grassroots level
  • Provide exposure to local players at a bigger stage
  • Maintain discipline, sportsmanship & international-level standards
  • Build a strong cricketing culture in the Northeast region
🌟

Our Vision

To become the biggest and most respected franchise-based cricket league in Northeast India, creating opportunities for players to reach state, national, and international levels.

  • Make NECPL a recognized cricket brand across India
  • Attract sponsors and professional franchises
  • Organize high-standard tournaments every season
  • Inspire youth to choose sports as a career

Tournament Rules
Playing Regulations
Official NECPL rules for Season 1. All other regulations follow standard international cricket rules.
01
〰️

Wide Ball Rule

Each wide ball awards the batting team 2 Runs plus an extra ball to be bowled.

2 Runs + Extra Ball
02
🚫

No Ball Rule

Every no ball gives the batting team 2 Runs and the next delivery is a mandatory Free Hit.

2 Runs + Free Hit
03
💥

Hat-trick Sixes Bonus

If a batsman hits 3 consecutive sixes, they are awarded 3 Extra Bonus Runs on top of the 18 scored.

3 Sixes → +3 Bonus Runs
04
📋

Standard International Rules

All other rules follow standard ICC rules and regulations as applicable to T10/T20 formats.

ICC Standards Apply
